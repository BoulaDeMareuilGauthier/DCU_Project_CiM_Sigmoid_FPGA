Key Findings

  • Energy Efficiency: Adder-free achieves 98.7% energy savings compared to Full adder across all precisions
  • Area Efficiency: Adder-free achieves 94.0% area savings compared to Full adder
  • Latency Trade-off: Adder-free has higher latency than approximate adders but competitive with exact adders at higher precisions
  • Ideal for: Energy-constrained edge computing applications where power and area are critical
